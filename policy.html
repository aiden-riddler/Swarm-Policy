Privacy Policy

Swarm Messenger

Last Updated: January 28, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Swarm ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application Swarm (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. This App is designed with privacy in mind and operates on a peer-to-peer basis.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • ✅ No personal information
  • ✅ No contact details
  • ✅ No location data
  • ✅ No usage analytics
  • ✅ No device identifiers
  • ✅ No payment information
  • ✅ No third-party tracking

3. How Our App Works

Swarm is a peer-to-peer messaging application that uses Wi-Fi Direct technology to enable communication between nearby Android devices.

3.1 Local Network Communication

  • The App uses Wi-Fi Direct to establish connections between devices
  • All communication occurs directly between devices on the local network
  • No messages or data pass through our servers or any third-party servers

3.2 Data Storage

  • Messages are stored temporarily on your device during active sessions
  • Chat history may be stored locally on your device if you choose to save it
  • We do not have access to or store your messages on any external servers

4. Information Processed Locally

The following information is processed locally on your device:

4.1 Device Information

  • Device names and identifiers for connection purposes
  • Network information required for Wi-Fi Direct communication
  • Technical information necessary for establishing connections

4.2 Message Content

  • Text messages you send and receive
  • Audio data during voice calls (processed in real-time)
  • Connection status information

5. Permissions Required

The App requires the following permissions to function:

5.1 Required Permissions

Permission Purpose
Location Access Required for Wi-Fi Direct functionality (Android system requirement)
Wi-Fi & Network Access Essential for peer-to-peer communication
Microphone Access Required for voice call features
Storage Access Optional for saving chat history locally

5.2 Permission Usage

  • Location data is used only for Wi-Fi Direct discovery and is not stored or transmitted
  • Microphone access is used only during active voice calls
  • Network access is used exclusively for device-to-device communication

6. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect personal information. The App:

  • Contains no advertising networks
  • Uses no analytics frameworks
  • Includes no social media integrations
  • Employs no cloud services for data storage

7. Data Security

7.1 Local Security

  • All communications occur directly between devices
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Messages stay within your local network environment

7.2 Technical Measures

  • Wi-Fi Direct provides encrypted communication between devices
  • No central server means no central point of failure or data breach
  • You control which devices can connect to yours

8. Children's Privacy

Our App does not:

  • Collect personal information from anyone, including children
  • Require registration or age verification
  • Specifically target children as users

However, we recommend that children use the App under parental supervision due to the nature of peer-to-peer communication.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data:

  • There is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete
  • You have complete control over your local data
  • You can uninstall the App at any time to remove all local data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Not require your consent for changes that maintain our no-data-collection approach
